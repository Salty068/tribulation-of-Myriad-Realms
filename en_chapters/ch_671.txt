Chapter 671: Daofuse Success

Where was Su Yu?

Naturally, he was still traversing the River of Time!

At this moment, Su Yu was exhausted.

Utterly fatigued!

As he went against the current, he was constantly assaulted by waves. It felt like he had already traveled a great distance upstream, seeing many Great Dao tributaries along the way, but he still hadn't reached the pen dao!

The further he went, the fewer Great Daos there were.

Su Yu realized that to forge a Great Dao, one had to go further upstream.

Otherwise, the section of the River of Time one could tear open would be filled with tributaries, leaving no opportunity to forge a Great Dao, only to Daofuse.

Daofusing was a gamble.

A gamble that the original Lord of Laws who forged that Great Dao had died, leaving the path uncontrolled. Otherwise, one would never reach the Lord of Laws realm. Only by forging one's own Great Dao was there the greatest hope of becoming a Lord of Laws.

Su Yu continued upstream!

At this point, his 100 character combat techniques were also heavily depleted, but they still carried Su Yu forward.

*Boom!*

Another massive wave struck. Su Yu roared, transforming his pen into a blade, and unleashed a Heaven-splitting slash. With a thunderous crash, the wave shattered!

And just as this slash landed, a nearby tributary appeared.

Blade qi soared into the sky!
