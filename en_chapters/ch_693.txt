Chapter 693: Four Realms Pacified (Seeking Subscriptions)

Tens of thousands of Phoenix Race members were captured.

They brought with them fear, dread, hostility, and resentment.

Resentment soared to the heavens!

However, they were powerless, unable to change anything.

Su Yu didn’t look at them much. After watching the sky for a while, he suddenly chuckled, “I won’t kill you. I came here this time without killing a single person. I just hope for win-win cooperation. Slaughter is not the solution to problems.”

At this moment, Su Yu even released the willpowers of those Eternals, including the Phoenix Emperor’s.

These Eternals, including the Phoenix Emperor’s willpower, seeped out a little, allowing them to hear Su Yu speak.

Su Yu’s “Saint” character continued to activate, and he said with a gentle smile, “I came to resolve the Myriad Races War, not for mere slaughter. However, I also know that sweet talk is useless. I can only capture you all first before discussing peace!”

“If I wanted to destroy the Phoenix Race, I could do so right now!”

“But, extermination is not my wish!”

Su Yu said softly, “So, I’ll give you a choice. I can release one Eternal to manage the Phoenix Realm. The remaining Eternals, including the Phoenix Emperor, must be imprisoned! If not imprisoned, you yourselves know that you will likely soon become my great enemies!”
