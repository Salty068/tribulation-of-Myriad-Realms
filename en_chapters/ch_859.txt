Su Yu ignored them, looking towards the rear at the Human Emperor, transmitting his voice, "Deplete the other side, both sides are exhausted... I'll wait for you to return. You take the people at the rear to recuperate for a few days, then come back to assist in the battle... At that time, with experts in peak condition, we'll face the other side! I think we can definitely achieve great results!"

The Human Emperor thought for a moment and reminded, "Don't underestimate the myriad races. Those old guys are also ruthless! They know we must have a purpose if we keep fighting this war of attrition... Be careful, they might pull off something big!"

Something big?

Su Yu pondered for a moment, how could the other side pull off something big?

After thinking for a while, Su Yu transmitted his voice, "If they want to reverse the situation, it's nothing more than you coming and continuing the fight! Or... hitting me hard?"

Thinking of this, he was slightly moved, "They wouldn't want to blow up my domain, would they? Severely injure my domain?"

If that happened, Su Yu's side would see a significant drop in combat power!

The Human Emperor was also moved, transmitting his voice, "It's possible. The Immortal Emperor, Divine Emperor, these few, are ruthless. I also noticed that some of the myriad races are avoiding battle. These few might kill a chicken to warn the monkeys... or even plot against them, using their lives to exchange for your domain being severely injured!"

Su Yu judged that this possibility still existed.

If it truly comes to that, his world wouldn't be invincible. If too many powerful beings self-destructed, specifically targeting his world, Su Yu's world could be breached or severed from its connection to the Myriad Realms.

The Human Emperor asked, "When do you want me to leave?"

Su Yu glanced at the heavily injured experts; there were quite a few of them. Below the second tier, almost nine out of ten were severely injured. Of course, most of them were from the Human Emperor's side.

After some thought, Su Yu transmitted a message, "Wait a little longer. Leave when even the second-tier ones are too injured to fight."

The Human Emperor nodded.

Su Yu then shouted again, "Charge!"

A group of people, including those under Su Yu's command, were panting heavily from exhaustion. Even Fat Ball was sticking out its tongue in fatigue. They were battered, injured, and had to keep fighting relentlessly. Su Yu was a tyrant, a madman. This kind of fighting was driving even his own people to the brink of collapse!

The fact that the Myriad Races hadn't collapsed yet was entirely due to a few top-tier experts holding the line, maintaining an advantage in the first-tier battles. Otherwise, they would have surely fallen apart by now.

Even the battle-hardened Heavendoom and Martial Emperor were fighting to the point of wanting to vomit blood! Truly, it was too exhausting.

High-intensity battles, for top-tier experts, once a year would be enough. Now, they were fighting ten times a day. By now, everyone was utterly fatigued!

...

Boom!

The power of various Daos erupted once more. Both sides were entangled, growing weary of the endless fighting. Today marked the ninth battle.

In this battle, even the Immortal Emperor did not participate. Instead, he stayed behind to heal the injured. Yes, healing. During this time, the Immortal Emperor was also making some preparations, even discerning who was truly injured and who was faking it.

At this point, the Immortal Emperor and his allies knew that Su Yu and his group had a purpose, just as they did. It was a matter of who could outwit whom.

The number of severely injured experts continued to rise. The Immortal Emperor kept healing them, consuming a tremendous amount of resources. However, this had the benefit of stabilizing the front lines. Seeing that no one was dying, everyone felt more at ease. If they couldn't fight, they would just get severely injured and retreat from the front lines.

Both sides were losing members!

...

On this day, the tenth battle erupted once more. This time, the Human Emperor's shadow, along with some others, quickly vanished.

The Great Zhou King's fake character was used again. It was difficult to disguise as a powerful expert, but not so hard to disguise as some injured ones. Moreover, with the cooperation of Su Yu's world power, the Human Emperor and his group, led by Marquis Tongtian, quickly disappeared from the battlefield.

As he left, the Human Emperor was actually very worried. This was just the appetizer. When he severed his Dao, if the Myriad Races weren't foolish, they would surely launch an all-out assault. That would be Su Yu's greatest crisis!

"We must go and return quickly!"

The Human Emperor was anxious. They had to quickly merge with the world, allowing everyone to integrate their Daos within, sever the Human Emperor's Great Dao, become spirits of the world, and bring the injured experts back to fight again!

To rescue Su Yu!

Otherwise, Su Yu would face the same situation he did back then.

"Hurry!"

The Human Emperor shouted lowly, looking at the people behind him, he said heavily, "Su Yu and his people rescued us. Now that most of our people have retreated... once I sever my Dao, if the Myriad Races aren't foolish, they will attack even at a great cost! We... don't want to be deserters, nor will we be. I hope everyone quickly merges with the world and returns to aid immediately!"

Everyone was solemn, nodding in agreement.

But soon, someone laughed, "Your Majesty, this one... can really fight! And dares to fight! Ten battles in one day, I'm about to vomit from fighting... When Giant Axe and the others said he was crazy, I didn't believe it. Now I do! This guy is a bit of a freak, Your Majesty, I feel like you're not as crazy as he is..."

The Human Emperor chuckled, quickly shook his head, and sighed, "He's different... forget it, I won't say more, lest he causes trouble."

Su Yu was different; he had experienced too much death. This guy was resilient to the extreme. What were ten battles?

The Myriad Races would eventually be driven mad by him!
