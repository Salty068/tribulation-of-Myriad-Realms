Chapter 680: All Are Acting

"Martial Emperor, don't push your luck!"
In the void, Su Yu snapped, "Do you really think I can't do anything to you? You haven't fully unsealed yet; I'm just too lazy to waste energy dealing with you. If you keep this up, do you believe I'll gather all my strength and kill you first?"

The Martial Emperor snorted coldly, punching through the void. He didn't use much power of laws, as his power of laws was sealed. However, without needing any power of laws, a single punch from his mere phantom made the pen dao tremble!

"Kill me?"
The Martial Emperor chuckled eerily, "You can try!"

"Still not done, are you?"
Su Yu suddenly pulled out a seal. The seal burst forth with brilliant light, and the Martial Emperor's phantom paused. Su Yu scoffed, "Try attacking again? Do you recognize this?"

The Martial Emperor's phantom stopped its punch, looking at Su Yu, then at the large seal in his hand. After a long moment, he said coldly, "The Dao of Sanctification?"

Su Yu grunted.

"Good that you recognize it. Can your current self block this? I'm just too lazy to waste it on you! Don't actually think I can't do anything to you!"
