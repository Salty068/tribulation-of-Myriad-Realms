Su Yu glanced at it. Inside the seal were the sources of all paths, and at this moment, there were still some of Lantian's avatars, seemingly connecting to the power of the myriad paths in the River of Time.

Inside the seal, it seemed like a central control center.

Su Yu took out a cage, which returned to its normal size. The Heavenly Overseer Marquis was talking to Duobao. When the cage appeared, he looked around, startled. What was this place?

Here, he couldn't even sense the power of the Myriad Realms' fortune!

Su Yu didn't explain, looking at him, he directly said, "Stay here, don't think about escaping, you can't escape!"

"This is the core of my world. If you can escape, I'll consider you impressive!"

After saying this, Su Yu quickly added, "I have other matters to attend to. You can comprehend the Dao of Fortune in my world, but..."

Su Yu coldly said, "You are not allowed to integrate into my Dao of Fortune, otherwise, I'll kill you!"

If this guy integrated, he would draw away the power of fortune from the Myriad Realms, causing great trouble. The seal would break, and that was not the outcome Su Yu wanted.

Of course, without his permission, it would be difficult for the Heavenly Overseer Marquis to integrate even if he wanted to.

Su Yu continued, "However, you can comprehend it and leave your insights here! Rest assured, I won't kill you. Why would I kill you?"

Su Yu smiled faintly, "You are my key protection target. If you die, wouldn't I be even more troubled? Heavenly Overseer Marquis, enjoy my protection here!"

At this moment, the Heavenly Overseer Marquis didn't know what to say, only feeling despondent. Was the gap between him and Su Yu growing larger?

The core of the world, connected to what Duobao had just said, he already knew what this place was.

Su Yu had opened his own world!

He was breaking free from the constraints of the Myriad Realms!

The Heavenly Overseer Marquis remained silent for a moment before speaking, "Even if you control me, it won't matter. Once the fortune of the Myriad Realms dissipates, I will die, and everyone else will still advance!"

Su Yu squinted his eyes and smiled, "True, but... that's something far in the future. Do you think I will need that long to deal with my opponents?"

Alright, the Heavenly Overseer Marquis thought for a moment. Perhaps he really wouldn't need that long. This guy was becoming increasingly terrifying!
