Su Yu had been domineering from the Lower Realm to the Upper Realm. Sometimes, he truly envied Su Yu.

Soon, nearly thirty Councilor Tokens were in Su Yu's hands.

After obtaining them, Su Yu quickly said, "Lie Kong Marquis, are you leaving?"

On the mountain, the Space Ancient Beast Race's Lie Kong Marquis was stunned. Su Yu smiled, "Your partner asked me to save you. Are you leaving? If so, take your people with you! As for the Hou Race, they're too weak. If they don't leave, they'll really die! As for the Fate Clan, forget it, Tianming, protect yourself!"

At this, the myriad races turned green!

What was this?

He was directly taking people away!

Tiangu took a deep breath and said solemnly, "Let them go! If they want to leave, do it quickly!"

It's better if they leave, the myriad races will be cleaner!

It would be best if Tianming and Leibo also took their people and left, that would be ideal!

On the mountain, Lie Kong Marquis no longer hesitated, quickly leading a group of Space Ancient Beasts, flying out with some peculiarity. Just like that... they left?

So simple!

In the distance, a small group of the Hou Race looked towards their only Dao Integration expert, wondering if they should leave.

That Dao Integration expert hesitated, "There's news from the Lower Realm, our race..."

"The Hou Pseudo Emperor?"

Su Yu chuckled, "You think I killed him? Why would I? The Hou Pseudo Emperor is living quite comfortably now. Are you leaving or not? If not, I'm out of here!"

The Hou Race expert hesitated for a moment before gritting his teeth and roaring, "My clan, retreat!"

Leave now!

The colossal mountain of the Myriad Races seemed on the verge of collapse. If not now, then when?

Moments later, Su Yu led the experts of the two races away swiftly. Meanwhile, on the mountain, Fate looked awkward. "He did it on purpose. I have nothing to do with him!"

The crowd from Tiangu's side couldn't be bothered to respond. Did he really think they were blind?

Tiangu, however, was pondering what Su Yu was up to this time. Was he planning to unseal the Upper and Lower Realms?

"Unseal the Martial Emperor?"

He murmured, thoughts swirling in his mind. He quickly recalled Su Yu's previous words. Could it be that there was truly a grudge between him and Doubao?

Doubao had been consuming his obsessions for many years. Sometimes, things were just that strange. Since Doubao had always wanted to consume him, and this desire had persisted from ancient times to the present, there might indeed be something unknown at play.

Tiangu grew increasingly melancholic. In the future, perhaps a confrontation was inevitable. Was he destined to die in Doubao's maw? That was not an outcome he wished to see.

He stopped dwelling on it, feeling somewhat disinterested. As he walked, he said, "Once the Upper and Lower Realms open, Thunderstorm, you should take your people and leave."

Thunderstorm's expression shifted slightly, and he replied awkwardly, "Tiangu, my friend, those words..."

"If you don't leave... then stay!"

Tiangu's voice was calm, "The Myriad Races still have the strength to fight. Does Brother Thunderstorm truly believe we cannot annihilate the Giant Race?"

Thunderstorm was left speechless. After a long pause, he finally said, "And Fate..."

Marquis Fate glanced at him indifferently, as if to say, why do you care about me? I'll leave if I want to. If not, Tiangu can't do anything about it!

Tiangu was clearly more afraid of Su Yu. If he couldn't see that, he was a fool!
