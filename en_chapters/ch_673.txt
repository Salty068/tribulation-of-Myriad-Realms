Chapter 673: Replacing the Garrisons

The Human Realm had entered autumn.

The autumn wind rustled.

This was probably the last calm before the great war. The entire Human Realm knew that the Myriad Realms were at war.

There wasn't a full-scale war, but tens of millions of soldiers were constantly attacking minor realms. Any minor realms in the Eastern War Zone that refused to surrender would be annihilated!

Xia Longwu and several other generals led their armies, sweeping through like a plague of locusts!

Refuse to surrender, and your race would be exterminated!

Surrender, and you still had to offer up vast amounts of resources!

Xia Longwu and his forces were using countless resources, using the Myriad Realms to sustain humanity.

This was war!

There was no right or wrong. The minor races thought humanity was evil, humanity felt forced by the Myriad Races, and the Immortal and Devil races believed humanity's ambition was too great. It was a vicious cycle!

Su Yu didn't concern himself with these matters.

These were wars beneath the Eternal realm. He heard that in the army, in just two months, dozens of Sunmoon Realm cultivators and nearly a thousand Mountain Sea Realm cultivators had emerged, all forcibly elevated through countless resources and heavenly rewards.

A war of this scale across the Myriad Realms had only occurred once before, over four hundred years ago, and it hadn't been as intense as this.
