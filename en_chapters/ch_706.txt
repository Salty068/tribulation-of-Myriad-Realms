The West King's Consort continued with a smile, "Su Yu, don't think about killing all the time. If I hadn't been captured by you, I would have hoped for your death. But now, I wish for your survival. Otherwise, wouldn't I be in trouble?"

Su Yu glared at her for a moment before sneering, "Trying to manipulate me? I can't be bothered with you!"

With that, his figure vanished.

After he left, the West King's Consort exhaled lightly. Su Yu, that madman, actually intended to provoke a beast tide at Chaos Mountain. Could the corpse of a Desolate Beast really trigger it? She wasn't sure.

The West King's Consort felt a bit uneasy. Clearly, Su Yu had set his sights on Chaos Mountain. She hoped he would abandon this idea soon.

...

Outside, Su Yu's expression shifted slightly. The West King's Consort didn't want him to incite a beast tide at Chaos Mountain! Even when he claimed it was to eliminate the humans from the Upper Realm, she refused to give him the formation layout. The so-called ancestral teachings were just nonsense!

"What is she worried about? Is she concerned that the beast tide will affect the Control King's lineage?"

"If so, the Great Ming King's deduction might be correct. This lineage is indeed hiding among them!"

"When I mentioned a powerful figure capable of resisting the Immortal Battle Marquis, she wasn't surprised. That's a Heavenly King-level expert. Wouldn't it be shocking for the human race to have an unfamiliar Heavenly King-level expert? Yet, she didn't question it, only claiming not to know..."

"When I said Marquis Minshan was betrayed by her lineage, she denied it..."

Thoughts flickered through Su Yu's mind, and after a while, he was mostly certain. The Great Ming King's deduction was likely true. This lineage was indeed hiding in Chaos Mountain, and there were definitely Heavenly King-level experts among them. The West King's Consort believed the black-robed figure, which Su Yu had fabricated, was one of her kin, which was why she didn't reveal anything.

"I really want to kill her, devour her blood essence, and see her memories!"

At that moment, Su Yu truly considered killing the West King's Consort, but he knew that doing so might alert the Control King's lineage and make them wary.

...

The Great Ming King noticed Su Yu emerging, deep in thought, and didn't interrupt him. Only when Su Yu regained his senses did he ask, "How did it go?"

Su Yu took a deep breath, "The Great Ming King might be right. This lineage is likely in Chaos Mountain, and there are quite a few powerful figures, definitely including Heavenly King-level experts!"

The Great Ming King wasn't too surprised, as it was his own deduction. At this moment, he sighed, "They're planning to be the fisherman, aren't they? The myriad races and the human race have been fighting for years, and here they are, accumulating strength, slowly depleting the power of both sides, and then striking to seize control of the Myriad Realms... If that's the case, their ambition is not small!"

Su Yu's face was cold as he looked towards the depths of Chaos Mountain. After a while, he said, "If they weren't noticed, it would be fine. But now that they are, thinking they can reap the benefits as the fisherman won't be so easy!"

"This lineage must not have the absolute strength to control the Myriad Realms. Otherwise, they would have already made their move. They're still waiting. Since the West King's Consort went to contact the West King, it means their strength isn't enough! They might even hope for the Hundred Battle King to be unsealed and fight the myriad races again, depleting both sides' strength... If that's the case, then there's still time and opportunity!"

The Great Ming King nodded slightly, "That seems likely! But if so, staying here is quite dangerous."

"I know."

Su Yu nodded. However, this was the periphery, and the other side might have already abandoned it to avoid drawing attention, so the danger wasn't too great for now.

"Continue searching for the formation core, and put other matters aside for now. Also, don't disclose this information."

Su Yu instructed in a deep voice.

The Great Ming King nodded, not saying much. Spreading this information might not be beneficial. As for informing the myriad races, they might not believe it, and even if they did, it would require the right timing.
