Chapter 667: Arrival in the Five Elemental Realm

Allheaven Battlefield.

Above the Sea of Stars.

Su Yu's expression was calm. The Great Zhou King soon returned, and the Book Spirit and Tea Plant also quickly returned, standing to Su Yu's left and right. The Tea Plant transformed into a young girl, curiously observing the Great Zhou King, thinking, *This one is quite formidable!*

*Of course, one must also be wary.*

The Book Spirit had just sent a voice transmission, warning her to be careful of this one attacking the Human Lord.

At this moment, the Tea Plant curiously scrutinized the Great Zhou King. *Is he a bad guy?*

The Great Zhou King paid no mind to the two. He returned before Su Yu and softly said, "I failed to capture Jiantian Hou, Emperor Yu, please forgive me!"

Su Yu said indifferently, "If you didn't capture him, then you didn't. But... why did you lose the 'Record' character fragment?"

The Great Zhou King softly replied, "The other party tampered with that fragment, and we never noticed! I simply took this opportunity to give it to them, to let the myriad races' powerhouses see it and foster more suspicion. Emperor Yu rarely makes a losing deal, and even Jiantian Hou himself will feel somewhat uneasy..."

Su Yu looked at him, then after a moment, smiled and said, "Indeed! But... next time, remember to tell me in advance!"
