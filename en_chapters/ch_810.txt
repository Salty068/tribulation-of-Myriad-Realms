Su Yu chuckled, "Of course, it depends on their judgment! Maybe they think I'm easier to deal with, and they'll choose to fight me to the death. I don't mind that!"

The Great Zhou King now fully understood Su Yu's intentions!

Su Yu wanted to drive wolves to devour tigers!

The key was, it was still very difficult.

Moreover, the Gate of Hell was extremely dangerous. Would the Myriad Races choose to kill the Sin Clan, break into the Gate of Hell for a slim chance of survival, or fight Su Yu to the death?

This was something even Su Yu couldn't be sure of.

"But didn't Your Majesty say you wouldn't let the Myriad Races interfere with Hundred Battle's fight against the Sin Clan?"

"Of course!"

Su Yu laughed, "The Myriad Races won't harass Hundred Battle but will deal with the Sin Clan. That doesn't conflict with what I said! What do you think?"

The Great Zhou King thought for a moment and nodded.

Su Yu continued, "It's a dog-eat-dog world, and we're not going to get involved in their fight, are we? That would be a waste of time! Hundred Battle should be thanking me for lending him a hand!"

The Great Zhou King wasn't sure if Hundred Battle would be grateful, but he knew that if he were, he'd probably be thanking Su Yu's entire family!

Su Yu was once again ready to act inhumanely!

He was an expert at exploiting the virtuous!

At this moment, looking at Hundred Battle, it was clear that he was indeed much kinder than Su Yu. Su Yu truly had a heart of darkness!
