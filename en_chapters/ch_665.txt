Chapter 665: Monarchs Use Words, Not Hands

Death Realm.

The West Heavenly King was in full retreat!

At this moment, the West Heavenly King was growing anxious; trouble had arrived, this dog was too strong!

The next moment, he shouted, "North Heavenly King, if I die today, you'll be next! You should know the consequences!"

The South Heavenly King had indeed blocked him!

However, the North Heavenly King still had many Death Marquises under his command, even more than the South Heavenly King. The South Heavenly King sided with the Human Race, so he had fewer Death Marquises. If the North Heavenly King joined the battle, even if he himself was blocked by the South Heavenly King, sending three to five Death Marquises would still create an opportunity!

Otherwise, there would truly be no chance!

To the north.

The North Heavenly King's projection wavered, caught in a struggle. The next moment, he no longer hesitated. The West Heavenly King was right; if he didn't join the battle now and the West Heavenly King died, his own days wouldn't be good either.

He feared the West Heavenly King and the West King's Consort, but he didn't wish for them to die just like that.

If the balance was broken, could he still expect good days?
