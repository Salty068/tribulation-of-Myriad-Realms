At this moment, Tiangu suddenly opened his eyes, saying indifferently, "Yuan Sheng has become a Heavenly Sovereign. My combat strength won't be affected, equivalent to our clan gaining another Heavenly Sovereign! Now, the survival of the Immortal Clan is at stake. It's not as simple as you think! The real threat of extinction comes from Su Yu, not the Control King's lineage! Believe it or not, this is my governing strategy moving forward!"

"Guard against Su Yu. Be cautious in all matters related to him. Think thrice before acting!"

Boom!

A burst of energy erupted as Yuan Sheng's aura instantly strengthened to the level of a Heavenly Sovereign. Opening his eyes, he looked at Tiangu with a complex expression. "You..."

Tiangu said coldly, "I don't need your gratitude. It's too cheap! From now on, don't oppose me. Obedience is the best outcome! The Immortal Emperor's Great Dao isn't omnipotent. It's more about understanding life! This path can't break through to become the master of rules. That might be the price! Of course, discussing this now is premature!"

Yuan Sheng was speechless. After a long time, he bowed, saying, "Yuan Sheng... understands! I will follow His Majesty's orders!"

At this moment, he chose to submit.

Because Tiangu was not quite what he had imagined.

At this moment, although Tiangu's strength had declined, the three Heavenly Sovereigns all nodded slightly. 

Below, the sound of submission quickly rose.

"We obey His Majesty's command!"

The voice resounded through the Immortal Race!

On his first day in the Upper Realm, Tiangu instantly took control of the entire Immortal Race.

Similarly, the former Empress swiftly regained control over the Divine Race. On the Demon Race's side, Demon Halberd and a few others argued their case, hoping to assert their influence within the Demon Race.

It was a unified will: the crisis of the Control King's lineage was not as severe as the crisis posed by Su Yu!
