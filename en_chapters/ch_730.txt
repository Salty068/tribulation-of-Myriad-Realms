As for the Fate Emperor, he didn't mention it. When Marquis Destiny meets with me, we'll discuss these matters.

The Fate Emperor was self-aware and didn't speak up. He hadn't contributed much and joined Su Yu late, so he wasn't as worthy of cultivation as the Iron Devourer Race.

Su Yu continued, "The Dragon Race's Heavenly Dragon Marquis and other strong experts have been personally suppressed by me and will be taken to the Upper Realm! The three major realms are currently sealed. This time, I will leave a few Daofuse experts to guard them, and the rest, I will take to the Upper Realm as appropriate... Of course, the foundation is third-tier Daofuse! Preferably second-tier Daofuse, as those too weak aren't needed!"

"......"

Below, someone finally couldn't hold back. Heavendoom couldn't help but ask, "What about me..."

Su Yu cast a sidelong glance at him. "You're only at the fifth-tier Daofuse Realm. Too weak. If you can reach the third-tier Daofuse this time, I'll take you with me. Otherwise... you better stay behind in the Myriad Realms!"

Heavendoom's face turned purple. How could this be? Once again, he was being left behind!

Fuming with indignation, he glanced at Grandstar, who remained calm and composed. In a low voice, Grandstar transmitted, "Don't bother looking. I'll be going up. I was already at the fourth-tier before, and after comprehending the Great Dao Diagram, I'll soon reach the third-tier... Heavendoom, it seems you'll be eating my dust again!"

"......"

I want to bash your head in with a stick!

Heavendoom was on the verge of madness, utterly frustrated. He couldn't accept it! Grandstar always managed to get ahead of him, whether it was becoming the city lord or ascending to the Upper Realm. Now, Grandstar's strength had even surpassed his own. It was simply too much!

This time, Grandstar had indeed gained a lot from the Upper Realm. He had absorbed a significant amount of the power of laws, comprehended the Great Dao Diagram, and participated in several battles involving the Heavenly Kings. In short, his gains far exceeded those from the Lower Realm.

Now, catching up to him was no easy task for Heavendoom. Grandstar, though outwardly calm, was inwardly smug. He transmitted again, "Stay here and guard the Lower Realm. I'll go up and kill a few, record the footage, and bring it back for you to watch."

Go to hell!

Heavendoom was losing his mind!

Meanwhile, Su Yu said nothing more. Streams of the power of laws flew towards the various Daofuse cultivators according to their merits. Those who had followed him up previously did not receive any more.

In the Lower Realm, June, Doubao, and Hongmeng received noticeably more than others, as did the Great Xia King and the Great Qin King. The guardians also received a fair share, with Heavendoom getting quite a bit.

After distributing everything, Su Yu's Mark of Civilization appeared, and the book cover crafted from the Great Dao Diagram was displayed before everyone. With a wave of his hand, the entire hall was enveloped in black and white.

"Everyone, observe the Dao well!" Su Yu's voice resonated like a great bell. "This is the Human Emperor's comprehension of the Great Dao, along with some of my own insights. If you gain understanding and have enough power of laws, you can quickly convert it into your own strength!"

"For now, only the Daofuse cultivators will be rewarded. Those below Daofuse will be rewarded next time based on their contributions!"

With those words, Su Yu floated out, leaving the others to sit cross-legged, each with a look of excitement. The Great Dao Diagram of the Human Emperor was something that even Daofuse cultivators in ancient times might not have had the privilege to observe, likely only the masters of laws could, and not even in its entirety. Now, they were fortunate enough to see it.

...

Outside the Emperor Yu Hall, Su Yu exhaled, glancing back with hope that these people would gain something. The pressure was still there. Returning to the Upper Realm, if the situation changed, another great battle might erupt.

He still needed to take some people to walk the River of Time, to see it firsthand, as the true River of Time couldn't be seen in the Upper Realm.

Beside him, Lantian appeared with a giggle, no longer the old man from before but a familiar little girl, laughing, "Brother Emperor Yu, do you think Brother Tiansheng can become a Heavenly King this time?"

Su Yu was speechless but accustomed to it. He smiled, "Hard to say! He's opening the Human Dao, and recently, I think he's been comprehending the Dao of Death. Maybe he has a chance, maybe not. It depends on luck!"

Wan Tiansheng was indeed a prodigy. The Human Dao was a powerful path, but his foundation was still somewhat lacking. Whether he could advance to a Heavenly King, Su Yu couldn't predict.

Lantian giggled, "He'll definitely make it. Brother Tiansheng is a freak, and freaks can go further!"

"......"

There was no way to respond to that!

Su Yu ignored him and looked towards the distant Great Zhou King, smiling, "Let's go, Great Zhou King. Let's take a trip along the River of Time. Let me see if the two paths of endurance can merge. If not... your importance will decrease significantly if you don't enter the Heavenly King domain!"

The Great Zhou King nodded, feeling a sense of crisis. It was over! He had to advance to a Heavenly King! Otherwise, in this era filled with prodigies, even his scheming wouldn't be needed. Wan Tiansheng and others were gradually taking over his role, and that wouldn't do!

In an instant, the River of Time was torn open, and the two of them entered it immediately.
